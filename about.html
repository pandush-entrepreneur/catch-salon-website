Our Story

Built for Women Who Deserve More.

Catch Salon & Spa was born from a simple belief: that every woman in Spring Hill deserves access to luxury treatments, real results, and a team that genuinely cares about her.

Meet Our Team
Who We Are

This isn't just a salon. It's a sanctuary.

Catch Salon & Spa was founded by Andrea Harbison with one vision: to create a space where luxury and innovation live side by side. A place where you can come in feeling exhausted, self-conscious, or simply ready for a change, and leave feeling like the most confident version of yourself.

We are not a volume salon. We are not a cookie-cutter med spa. We are a boutique destination where every treatment is personalized, every detail is intentional, and every client leaves with a plan.

We're nestled in Spring Hill, TN, but our clients come from all across the Nashville area. They're drawn by the results, the experience, and a team that treats every single person who walks through our door like a priority.

From advanced laser treatments and body contouring to luxury facials and precision hair color, Catch brings together the full spectrum of beauty and wellness under one beautifully designed roof.

This is where you come to catch the best version of yourself.

The Catch Team

The people behind your transformation.

Founder · Owner · Stylist

Andrea Harbison

Andrea Harbison is the visionary behind Catch Salon & Spa. The experience feels the way it does from the moment you walk in because of her.

With over a decade of experience in the beauty industry, Andrea has built Catch from the ground up as Spring Hill's premier destination for luxury hair, skin, and body treatments. Her eye for detail, her passion for artistry, and her relentless commitment to excellence are woven into every corner of the Catch experience: from the treatment menu to the way the space looks and feels.

Andrea believes that beauty services should do more than make you look good. They should make you feel seen, cared for, and genuinely transformed. That philosophy shapes everything Catch does.

When she's not behind the chair or building the next chapter of Catch, you'll find her chasing inspiration and setting a standard that Spring Hill hasn't seen before.

Licensed Esthetician · Skin Specialist

Megan

Megan brings over a decade of hands-on aesthetics experience to the Catch team, along with licenses in California, Texas, and Tennessee that reflect just how seriously she takes her craft.

Since earning her esthetics license in 2013, Megan has developed a reputation for delivering treatments that combine advanced technique with genuine warmth. She specializes in customized facials and body contouring services, with a philosophy rooted in both immediate results and long-term skin health. Her clients don't just leave glowing. They leave with a plan.

What sets Megan apart is the way she makes every guest feel: informed, comfortable, and completely confident that their skin is in the right hands. Whether she's performing RF Microneedling, body contouring, or a deeply customized facial, her approach is always personal, always precise, and always results-driven.
