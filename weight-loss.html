Medical Weight Loss

The weight loss breakthrough you've been waiting for.

Semaglutide is a clinically proven, FDA-recognized injectable medication that has transformed the way medical professionals approach weight management. Originally developed to treat type 2 diabetes, it was quickly recognized for something remarkable: its ability to produce significant, sustained weight loss.

Here's how it works: Semaglutide acts on receptors in the brain that regulate appetite and fullness, naturally reducing hunger and food intake without willpower battles or crash dieting. The result is steady, meaningful weight loss that's supported by your body's own biology.

At Catch, your Semaglutide program is managed by our Nurse Practitioner, who will review your full health history, current medications, and personal goals before building a treatment plan designed specifically for you.

Clinically Proven Results

Semaglutide has demonstrated significant reductions in body weight in clinical trials, making it one of the most effective non-surgical weight loss tools available today.

Simple Weekly Injections

No complicated pill schedules. No daily routines to manage. One simple weekly injection, administered with full guidance from our team.

Beyond the Scale

Alongside weight loss, Semaglutide may help improve metabolic markers like blood sugar and blood pressure, supporting your overall health, not just the number on the scale.

Expert Support, Every Step

From your first consultation through your ongoing treatment, our Nurse Practitioner is with you. She adjusts your plan as you progress and keeps your safety and success at the center of everything.

Important: Semaglutide is a prescription medication. A consultation with our Nurse Practitioner is required before beginning treatment. Individual results may vary. Best results are achieved alongside a balanced diet and regular physical activity.
