For clients ready to go further, our laser services represent the pinnacle of non-surgical skin correction. Whether you're looking to address deep wrinkles, stubborn acne scars, or early signs of aging, we have the technology and the expertise to deliver dramatic, lasting improvement.

CO2 Laser

Deep resurfacing for significant wrinkle reduction, acne scarring, and skin aging. Often one treatment delivers major improvement. Typical recovery: 5 to 10 days.

MOXI Laser

A gentler fractional laser ideal for early aging, sun damage, pigmentation, and brides wanting a luminous glow with minimal downtime of 1 to 3 days.

Body Contouring

Body Contouring

Non-surgical. Results-driven. Technology-based sculpting.

Catch Your Body Goals

At Catch, you don't have to choose between fat reduction and skin tightening. We combine technologies strategically to address both. Our body contouring program is built around your specific goals, not a one-size-fits-all machine.

Cryo (Cryo T-Shock)

Alternating hot and cold temperatures target and break down stubborn fat cells in areas like the lower abdomen, flanks, and inner thighs. No surgery, no anesthesia, no downtime.

Freeze stubborn fat. Target what workouts won't.

Sculptor (Vacuum + RF)

Vacuum-assisted radio frequency tightens skin, stimulates collagen, improves circulation, and smooths the appearance of cellulite. All without surgery.

Tighten without surgery. Stimulate collagen naturally.

Liposonix (HIFU)

High-intensity focused ultrasound penetrates deep fat layers to permanently destroy targeted fat cells. A powerful next step for clients seeking stronger, longer-lasting results.

EMS

Build definition, tone the core, and enhance the results of your fat-reduction treatments. Electrical Muscle Stimulation that works hard so you don't have to.
