Non-Surgical Lift

Non-Surgical Facelift (HIFU)

Turn back the clock. No surgery required.

High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound (HIFU) is one of the most powerful non-invasive skin-tightening treatments available today. The ultrasound energy penetrates deep into the skin's layers, generating controlled heat that triggers collagen production, firms sagging tissue, and promotes long-lasting lift and rejuvenation. Results can often be seen immediately.

Advanced Skin

RF Microneedling

Collagen, rebuilt from the inside.

Microneedling combined with Radio Frequency energy is one of our most sought-after anti-aging treatments. Tiny micro-channels in the skin's surface trigger the body's natural healing response, while RF energy heats the deeper dermal layers. Together they stimulate powerful collagen and elastin production that firms, smooths, and dramatically improves skin texture over time.

Best for Fine lines + wrinkles  ·  Acne scarring  ·  Skin laxity  ·  Enlarged pores  ·  Texture

Cryo Facial

Cryotherapy Facial

Cool down. Lift up. Light up.

A controlled blast of cold air delivered to the skin causes blood vessels to constrict, instantly reducing inflammation, puffiness, and redness. As the skin warms back up, circulation surges and collagen production is stimulated, leaving you with a visibly tighter, lifted, and glowing complexion. A favorite for event prep and a powerful add-on to any treatment.

Best for Puffiness  ·  Redness  ·  Pre-event  ·  Collagen boost  ·  Glow enhancement

Light Therapy

LED Therapy Facial

Light that heals. Skin that glows.

LED light therapy delivers precise wavelengths of energy deep into the skin to stimulate collagen repair, smooth fine lines, and address multiple skin concerns simultaneously. Red light rebuilds and firms; blue light targets acne-causing bacteria for clearer, healthier skin. Non-invasive, completely comfortable, and deeply effective.

Best for Fine lines  ·  Active acne  ·  Post-treatment recovery  ·  Overall skin health

IPL

IPL Photofacial (Intense Pulsed Light)

Fade spots. Even your tone. Reveal clarity.

IPL photorejuvenation uses precise pulses of light to penetrate deep into the skin and target pigmentation, sun damage, broken capillaries, and acne scars without affecting the surrounding skin. The result is a visibly clearer, more even, and more luminous complexion with minimal downtime.

Best for Dark spots  ·  Sun damage  ·  Rosacea  ·  Uneven skin tone  ·  Acne scarring
