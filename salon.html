Keratin Treatment

Frizz-free. Healthy. Half the styling time.

Our Keratin treatment uses KERABOND™ Technology to smooth the hair, reduce curl up to 95%, and leave your hair healthy, shiny, and completely manageable, with significantly fewer fumes than traditional keratin treatments. Results last up to three months, and most clients cut their daily styling time in half. If you've been fighting your hair every morning, this is the treatment that changes that.

Best for: Frizzy or unruly hair · Curl reduction · Shine and manageability · Saving time
